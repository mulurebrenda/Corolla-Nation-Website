About us

Corolla Owners Club

Corolla Nation is a vibrant community of Corolla owners and enthusiasts who are passionate about the capabilities of this iconic automobile.

Tradition & History

The club was founded on August 2nd, 2021 by Charles Mulure and Adnan Shiraz and has grown significantly since its inception, with over 130 members and 20 affiliate members who share the same passion. The club organizes annual events like charity drives, fun drives, and local meetups mostly in Nairobi, where members can come together and share their love for the Corolla. These events are not just for fun and leisure but also for charity, as the club aims to support the people around them and make an impact in the nation.

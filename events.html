Events

Events

At the end of every year we always put out a calendar for the next year's events. These are annual events like charity drives, fun drives, and local meetups mostly in Nairobi, where members can come together and share their love for the Corolla.

March 29

Easter Holiday in Uganda

Join us for a joyous and unforgettable Easter celebration in the heart of Uganda! Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture, rich traditions, and the spirit of togetherness as we mark this special occasion from March 29 to April 1, 2024. Come one, come all, and let's make this Easter Holiday an experience to cherish!

Event Schedule:

Date: March 29 - April 1, 2024

Activation Meet: March 10, 2024

June 1

Mount Kenya Loop

Join us for the exhilarating Mount Kenya Loop adventure on June 1-2, 2024! This two-day event promises an unforgettable journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Mount Kenya. Before embarking on this thrilling expedition, we will gather for an activation meet where we will receive essential information about the event.

Event Schedule:

Date: June 1 - 2, 2024

Activation Meet: May 18, 2024

August 3

Kericho Anniversary Charity Run

This memorable event is set to take place on August 3-4, 2024. The event isn't just about the thrill of the run; it's about making a positive impact. The Kericho Anniversary Charity Run aims to raise funds for local charitable initiatives, contributing to the betterment of our community.

Event Schedule:

Date: August 3 - 4, 2024

Activation Meet: July 28, 2024

October 19

Malindi Coast Drive

Join us for an unforgettable road trip adventure to Malindi and enjoy breathtaking landscapes, coastal vistas, and cultural wonders that the Malindi has to offer. Get ready to rev up your engines and create lasting memories. Malindi Coast Drive awaits - an adventure of a lifetime!

Event Schedule:

Date: October 19 - 21, 2024

Activation Meet: October 13, 2024

December 15

Family Fun Day

Join us for a delightful day of family bonding and joy at our eagerly anticipated Family Fun Day. From engaging games and crafts to delicious treats and heartwarming moments, this event promises an unforgettable experience for everyone. Save the date for a day filled with laughter, love, and endless family fun!

Event Schedule:

Date: December 15, 2024

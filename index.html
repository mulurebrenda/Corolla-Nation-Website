ABOUT

Corolla Nation is a vibrant community of Corolla enthusiasts who are passionate about the capabilities of this iconic automobile. Founded on August 2nd, 2021 by Charles Mulure and Adnan Shiraz, the club has grown significantly since its inception, with over 130 members and 20 affiliate members who share the same passion. The club organizes annual events like charity drives, fun drives, and local meetups mostly in Nairobi, where members can come together and share their love for the Corolla. These events are not just for fun and leisure but also for charity, as the club aims to support the people around them and make an impact in the nation. At Corolla Nation, it's not just about cars, it's about the journeys we take together, from scenic drives along coastal roads to conquering mountain passes. It's a family outside of home.
In the heart of Corolla Nation Club beats the rhythm of the iconic Corolla, a symbol of reliability, innovation, and driving excellence. Whether you own a classic AE86 or the latest model, you're part of a legacy that spans generations.
